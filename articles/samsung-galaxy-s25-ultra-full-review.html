Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra: Full Review

Published on: 2025-02-16

Introduction: Let's talk about Samsung's new Galaxy S25 series. People say that there are no major updates in it, but let's find out which updates are in S25. The design looks similar to S24 but less sharp-edged. S25 Ultra introduces significant design, performance, and user experience improvements.

Design

Galaxy S25 Ultra has a refined design with more rounded edges. It looks slimmer and lighter compared to previous models. It comes with a durable titanium body, providing durability and elegance. It has a 6.9-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display with reduced bezels, which delivers an immersive viewing experience. Gaming enthusiasts will love the smooth 120Hz refresh rate, making gameplay ultra-responsive. The display is protected with Corning glare-free glass, ensuring clarity even in sunlight.

Performance

Compared to the S24 Ultra, this model offers 20% better AI performance and 15% improved power efficiency. The Galaxy S25 Ultra has Qualcomm's Snapdragon 8 Elite processor, ensuring swift and efficient performance. The device has 12GB RAM, capable of handling multitasking, gaming, and demanding applications easily. Samsung's advanced AI enhances user interactions and makes daily tasks easier.

Camera

Rear Camera: 200MP wide + 50MP ultra-wide + 50MP telephoto (5x optical zoom) + 10MP telephoto (3x optical zoom)

Front Camera: 12MP

Samsung really did a good job in camera setup. The 200MP wide camera captures ultra-detailed pictures with the best clarity. The 50MP ultra-wide camera is perfect for landscape shots. The 50MP and 10MP telephoto lenses provide excellent zoom capabilities for distance and mid-range shots. The 12MP front camera delivers sharp and vibrant selfies. There is no comparison with Samsung's camera quality.

Battery & Charging

S25 Ultra has a 5000mAh battery, providing power for a full day of intensive use. The device supports 45W fast charging, allowing users to quickly recharge and get back to their activities. It also supports wireless charging for a cable-free experience.

Software Updates

This device runs on Samsung's One UI 7, based on Android 15, providing a seamless and user-friendly interface. Samsung offers 7 years of security and software updates, ensuring users enjoy the latest features for a long time.

S Pen

This device comes with an S Pen, making it unique compared to other smartphones. With the S Pen, users can take notes, make sketches, or use it for precise navigation. It adds extra creativity and seamlessly integrates with the phone, allowing smooth switching between touch and pen input.

1TB: $1419.99
